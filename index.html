Welcome to ChaChingas!

Your Personal Financial Coach.

Are you a broke high schooler or college student trying to save for your future? A hardworking parent juggling bills and debt? Or someone who wants to start investing but has no idea where to begin? Wherever you’re starting from, ChaChingas has your back.

ChaChingas is a smart, friendly chatbot that helps you become more financially literate without needing a finance degree or a dictionary. We break down the basics of budgeting, saving, investing, credit, and smart spending into advice you can actually use, whether you’re just getting started or just trying to stop impulse-buying.

Here's what ChaChingas can help you with:

  • Budgeting Made Simple – set your financial goals, automate your savings, and we’ll help you figure out how much to save and spend each day and how to make guilt-free purchases.
  • Understanding The Terms – from what a mutual fund is to why credit scores matter, we keep it real and readable.
  • Investing 101 – learn about stocks, bonds, index funds, and how to start investing without feeling overwhelmed.
  • Credit & Debt Tips – boost your score, pay off loans, and avoid falling into the minimum-payment trap.
  • Help for Students and Families – from managing student accounts to finding low-income programs and understanding student loans.
  • Entrepreneurship Support – get support to turn your hustle into a real business.
